The alpha version of Quantum Launch, due for publication in 2027, is now out. This means several things.

1) An updated website, including blog comments, the third book teaser, a hidden mini-game, and more. (and more frequent blog postings until release!)

2) A new mailing list. Join the mailing list on the website to receive periodic email updates on the upcoming third and final book of the series. Stay in the know! 

3) The alpha is in the hands of my earliest readers! It's not done. I still have much polishing to do before I get to the beta version, but the entire story is locked in. It's polish, polish, polish from here on out! 

The alpha version clocks in at about 62,000 words, and the finished product is targeting 71,000 words, so room to grow/improve as I work toward the beta and production release. You can expect this "prequel" of how Quotient started in 2027! Feel free to drop questions in the comments below!

Enjoy!
