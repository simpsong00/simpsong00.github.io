Quotient the Game! Explore Quotient HQ as described in the book The Quantum Contingent. You can also meet many of the characters from the book while exploring headquarters. You can also fly to and explore Oxford! As in any good interactive fiction game, there are puzzles. If you solve them all, you'll get 107 points

Quotient Headquarters was created with Inform and has IFID A8BCDC10-952A-48D7-A258-277BC72AEC72. To play a work like this one, you need an interpreter program: many are available, among them Gargoyle for MacOS, Linux or Windows; Spatterlight for MacOS; Windows Frotz or Windows Glulxe for Windows. Or you can play without downloading anything by following the 'Play In-Browser' link, using the Quixe interpreter. You'll need to have Javascript enabled on your web browser.
