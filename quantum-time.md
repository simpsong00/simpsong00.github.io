        <p>Jack and Sarah are back. Sibling spies for Quotient — the world's most covert independent agency — they've barely had time to breathe after the events of <em>The Quantum Contingent</em>. Their vacation ends abruptly when a mysterious wind of death begins targeting President O'Connor and world leaders, and no one can explain it.</p>

        <p>What follows is a race across two timelines — 2028 and 2050 — as the Quotient team fights to protect the present while unraveling a threat that reaches into the future. <em>Quantum Time</em> is a fast-paced adventure thriller with richly detailed real-world locations, a large cast of engaging characters, and tomorrow's technology woven into every chapter.</p>

        <p>New locations span Sydney and the Great Barrier Reef, the Dead Sea, Masada, Paris, Austin, Munich, Shanghai, and Bangalore — all places Gregory R. Simpson has visited personally, brought to life with the same authenticity that defined Book 1.</p>
