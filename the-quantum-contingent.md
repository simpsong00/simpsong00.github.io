        <p>It's 2028. The world is changing fast — quantum computing is no longer theoretical, cryptocurrency has reshaped global finance, and drone swarms have rewritten the rules of warfare. Jack and Sarah are sibling spies working for Quotient, a covert independent agency operating in the shadows of global intelligence. When a powerful group begins exploiting quantum capabilities in a race toward immortality, Jack and Sarah are dispatched across the globe to stop them — before the world reaches a point of no return.</p>

        <p>From the research corridors of MIT and the cobblestone lanes of Oxford to the crater floors of Tanzania and the rocket launchpads of Texas, <em>The Quantum Contingent</em> moves at a relentless pace through real-world locations and cutting-edge technology. Every piece of tech in the book is real. Every location is a place the author has visited himself.</p>
